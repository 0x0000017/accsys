Data Privacy Policy

Data Privacy Policy for ACCSYS


This Data Privacy Policy outlines the practices and procedures implemented by The Team Analytech to protect the privacy and security of personal data collected through our inventory and sales management system. We are committed to complying with the provisions of the Data Privacy Act of 2012.


1. Collection and Use of Personal Data

1.1 Personal Data Collected

In the course of using our inventory and sales management system, we may collect the following personal data from retail stores and their authorized personnel:

  • Username: Required for system login and identification.
  • Password: Encrypted and securely stored for user authentication.
  • Email Address: Required for communication, system notifications, and password recovery.
  • Full Name: Required for user identification and communication purposes.
  • Personal Address: Required, provided by the user for contact purposes.
  • Store Name: Required to associate the user with the respective store.
  • Store Address: Required for accurate store identification and location.
  • Picture: Optional profile picture for user identification purposes.
1.2 Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and process the aforementioned personal data for the following purposes: User authentication and access control to the inventory and sales management system. Communication with users regarding system updates, notifications, and support. Accurate identification and association of users with their respective stores. Personalization of user experience within the inventory and sales management system. Compliance with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.


2. Data Protection and Security Measures

2.1 Confidentiality and Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and security of personal data collected. These measures include but are not limited to:

  • Secure transmission and storage of personal data using encryption protocols.
  • Regular security assessments and updates to safeguard against unauthorized access.
  • Restricted access controls and permissions to limit data access to authorized personnel.
  • Employee training on data protection and security best practices.

2.2 Third-Party Data Processors

We may engage third-party service providers to assist with data processing activities. These providers will be bound by contracts or agreements to ensure the protection and security of personal data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.


3. Data Retention and Disposal

We retain personal data for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Personal data will be securely disposed of in a manner that prevents unauthorized access or disclosure.


4. User Rights and Consent

4.1 User Consent

By using our inventory and sales management system, users consent to the collection, processing, and storage of their personal data as described in this Data Privacy Policy. Users have the right to withdraw their consent at any time by contacting us at teamanalytech@gmail.com. However, withdrawing consent may result in the inability to access certain features or services provided by the system.

4.2 User Rights

Users have the following rights regarding their personal data:

  • Right to Access: Users can request access to their personal data and receive information on how it is being processed.
  • Right to Rectification: Users have the right to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Users have the right to request the restriction or limitation of the processing of their personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Users can request the deletion or removal of their personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Data Portability: Users can request the transfer of their personal data to another organization in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights or make inquiries about the processing of personal data, users may contact us teamanalytech@gmail.com.


5. Developer Obligations

5.1 Confidentiality and Security

Developers who have access to personal data through the inventory and sales management system are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of the data. Developers must abide by strict data protection policies, sign confidentiality agreements, and undergo appropriate training to ensure compliance.

5.2 Data Usage Limitation

Developers are only authorized to access and process personal data for the purpose of maintaining, improving, or providing technical support for the inventory and sales management system. Any unauthorized use, disclosure, or sharing of personal data is strictly prohibited.

5.3 Data Retention and Disposal

Developers must adhere to data retention and disposal policies defined by our organization. Personal data should not be retained for longer than necessary and must be securely disposed of in accordance with applicable regulations.


6. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may pose a risk to the rights and freedoms of individuals, we will promptly notify affected individuals and the National Privacy Commission (NPC) in accordance with legal requirements.


7. Changes to the Data Privacy Policy

We may update this Data Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We encourage users to review this policy periodically for any updates.
